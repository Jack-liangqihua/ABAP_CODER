


PARAMETERS  p_chk AS CHECKBOX USER-COMMAND flag.


AT SELECTION-SCREEN OUTPUT.
  LOOP AT SCREEN.
    IF P_CHK = 'X' AND ( SCREEN-GROUP1 = 'GN' OR  SCREEN-GROUP1 = 'GN1' ).
      SCREEN-ACTIVE = 0 .
      MODIFY SCREEN .
    ENDIF.
    IF R2 <> 'X' AND SCREEN-GROUP1 = 'GN1'.
      SCREEN-ACTIVE = 0 .
      MODIFY SCREEN .
    ENDIF.
  ENDLOOP.
